This event is for youth to explore new ways to practice self care.

Join us for Shattering the Silence: Self Care Fair

Februray 16th, 2019
10am-6pm

This event is for youth to explore new ways to practice self care, including yoga, medieval longsword, art, gardening, compassion, story sharing, team building, qigong, aromatherapy, massage and much more. Attendees will walk away with new ideas on how to advocate for themselves and others, how to apply life skills to better manage mental health, and new ways to give back to our community.

Hosted by Imagine Zero and the Alliance for Suicide Prevention, and sponsored by Clear View Behavioral Health, UCHealth & Highlands Behavioral Health.
